Dripsuation Below

A 3D score-based game where players must keep a leaking submarine operational by repairing breaches and collecting water before it overwhelms the vessel. Empty your bucket into the torpedo launcher and fire the excess water away to survive as long as possible.

Open Itch.io Page

My Responsibilities

  • Developed interactive player tools, including the bucket and welding gun
  • Created dynamic water effects using Unity Shader Graph
