About Me

My name is Jesse Hirvonen, a game development enthusiast from Espoo, Finland with a background in game design, programming, and business information technology. I enjoy creating games through both technical and creative work, and over the years I have worked in several roles including game developer, level designer, narrative designer, and game artist. While I enjoy many areas of development, programming and problem-solving are where I feel most at home.

I graduated from Laurea University of Applied Sciences in 2022 with a Bachelor’s degree in Business Information Technology. Wanting to focus more on game development, I continued my studies in Game Design at HEO, where I graduated in 2023. I am currently studying Game Programming (Engineer, AMK) at Xamk, where I am continuing to deepen my skills in programming, software development, and game production.

I have also worked as a Junior Developer in a startup environment, where I gained practical experience in software development and collaborative workflows within a professional team. In addition to professional experience, I have participated in multiple game projects and published over 10 games on itch.io. Throughout these projects, I have worked in teams ranging from small groups of 3 people to larger teams of over 10 developers, which has helped me develop strong communication, teamwork, and version control skills in collaborative development environments.
